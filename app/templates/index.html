Why these targets?

To achieve Ketosis, your body must shift from burning glucose (sugar) to burning ketones (fat). This requires strict insulin management.

  • Net Carbs: These are carbs that impact blood sugar. Total Carbs minus Fiber = Net Carbs.
  • Protein: Set at 1g per kg of body weight to preserve muscle while keeping insulin stable.
  • Fat: Your energy source! Adjust fat intake to satisfy hunger once you've hit your protein goal.
Latest Literature: Aim for 7-10 cups (300g to 570g) of vegetables daily. These provide essential potassium and magnesium to prevent the "Keto Flu."
